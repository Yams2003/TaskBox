Guide and Manual

User Guide

To-Do List

The To-Do List is designed for users to set goals for themselves. This feature allows users to create goals they want to complete, users can update the progress of the task as they complete it.

Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Timer is a simple way to get users to stay focused while working. It works by switching between 25-minute work intervals and 5-minute breaks.

Journal

The Journal Page is designed to give a space for users to record their ideas and thoughts. A random prompt is presented to the users to stimulate their thoughts.

About Us

The About Us page is designed to add a sense of familiarity with the developers of the site. We want to make who we are and our intentions clear to the users.

References

The References page is set up to give users a list of top rated amazon books for staying motivated. Each image of the book is hyperlinked to the amazon page to give quick access for purchasing.
