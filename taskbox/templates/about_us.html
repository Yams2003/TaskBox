Our Vision

We strive to create a website with the potential to help people build healthy habits and further improve their lives. Users will ultimately interact with a to-do list that has a progress indicator and a habit tracker that shows them information regarding their interactions. As the list progresses to completion their virtual pet (which is an incentive) will become happier, signifying that the user has successfully accomplished their goals while creating the healthy habit of progressing through tasks.
